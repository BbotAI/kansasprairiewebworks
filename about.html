Why Kansas Prairie Webworks Exists

Five years ago, the owner of what would become Kansas Prairie Webworks was running a business called Diehls Trucking. Like a lot of small business owners, they needed a website and an online presence — and they didn't know how to build it themselves.

So they hired an out-of-state California agency. Paid $1,500 setup and $250 per month. And for a while, it seemed fine.

Then the problems started. The website was slow. The work was lazy. When they complained, the agency stopped answering. No calls returned. No emails. It took calling the bank directly to stop the automatic monthly charges.

"I paid over a thousand dollars to a company that ghosted me. I had nothing to show for it, no way to get my money back, and no idea how to fix it."

That experience lit a fire. Instead of just being angry, the owner started learning. Web development. SEO. AI tools. Automation systems. Crypto and blockchain platforms. Over several years of self-teaching and building real projects, the skills came together.

And one day the question became obvious: If I'd had someone local who actually answered the phone — who knew what Kansas businesses needed, who charged fair prices, and who stood behind their work — how different would that have been?

Kansas Prairie Webworks is the answer to that question.

"I'm not a California agency. I'm your neighbor."

We serve Salina, McPherson, Abilene, Bennington, Concordia, Junction City, Manhattan — and every small town in between where a business owner is trying to get found online without getting ripped off.

Every client gets direct access. Real answers. Honest pricing. And the absolute guarantee that your domain, your Facebook page, your Google profile — they belong to you, not us, forever.
