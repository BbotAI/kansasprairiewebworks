Common Questions

Do I need to provide content or do you write it?

We write everything. The intake form gives us what we need to write content that sounds like you and serves your customers. You review it before anything gets published — if it needs changes, we revise.

Can I bundle Blog + Facebook + Google Manager?

Yes. Blog writing is $100/month (Tier 3 add-on, works with any tier). Full social media posting across Facebook and Google is included in the Top Tier at $350/month, or available as a standalone add-on — contact us for pricing. Ask about bundling on your free call.

What if I don't like a post?

You always review content before it goes live. If you don't like it, tell us what to change and we'll revise. After a couple of months, we dial in your voice and preferences. Most clients reach a point where content requires little or no revision.

Can I cancel any time?

Yes. Monthly posting is month-to-month with no contracts. Cancel any time with 30 days notice. All published content stays live — it's yours. We never delete published blog posts or social posts when you cancel.
