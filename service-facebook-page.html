Common Questions

Do I need a personal Facebook account?

Yes — Facebook Business Pages are tied to personal accounts. If you don't have one, we'll walk you through creating a simple personal account that you can keep completely private. Your customers will only ever see the business page, not your personal profile.

What if I cancel your service — do I keep my page?

Yes, 100%. Your Facebook Business Page is always yours. We're added as an admin so we can work on it, but you remain the Page Owner. If you cancel for any reason, we remove ourselves as admins within 48 hours. Nothing disappears. Nothing is deleted. The page stays live under your account.

Will you be posting to my Facebook page too?

Setup only includes the initial 2 posts to get the page looking active. If you want ongoing posting, check out our Facebook Manager add-on service — we post 4–8 times per month with local-relevant content. See our Monthly Posting service page for details and pricing.

What if I already have a page but can't log in to it?

This is more common than you'd think — old email address, a former employee set it up, or you just can't remember the login. Facebook has a business page ownership claim process. We'll help you navigate it. If account recovery fails, we can set up a new page and migrate your content where possible.
