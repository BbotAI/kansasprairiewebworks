How the Pipeline Works

Six steps. You do two of them. We handle the rest — every week, every month, indefinitely.

1

You Fill Out One Form

When you sign up we email you a personalized intake link. You tell us about your business — your services, your service area, your tone, your goals. Takes about 15 minutes. You never fill it out again.

2

You Upload Your Images

Your client portal lets you upload photos organized by service category. Demolition, septic, lawn care, fencing — whatever your business does. Our system indexes every image with your description and tags it to the correct service automatically. The right photo gets used for the right post, every time.

3

KPW Legacy Brain Learns Your Business

Every detail you provide — services, locations, seasonal patterns, past posts, keywords — gets stored in your KPW Legacy Brain. Every piece of content we create from that point forward draws from it. Your business data works for you indefinitely, getting smarter with every cycle.

4

AI Generates and Grades Your Topics

Our system runs a geographic SEO keyword scrape for what customers in your area are actively searching. It generates 25 content topics, weights them by local search demand in Salina, Saline County, and Central Kansas, and selects the top 3 for your next content cycle.

5

Content Is Written, Optimized, and Queued

The top topics are passed to our content engine, which writes SEO-marked-up posts using your Legacy Brain data — your services, your images, your geographic keywords. Posts are prepared for Google Business, Facebook, and your blog on a consistent weekly and monthly schedule.

6

You Review. We Publish. You Move On.

Before anything goes live you get a review link. Approve it, request a change, or pass on it. Once approved we handle publishing, image pairing, captions, and scheduling. You run your business. We keep you visible.

Common Questions

Do I have to keep filling out forms every month?

No. You fill out the intake form once when you sign up. After that, our system draws from your KPW Legacy Brain data. You only interact when we send you content to review or when your business changes.

Is the content actually about my business or is it generic?

It is built from your intake form, your uploaded images, your service descriptions, and your geographic area in Central Kansas. It references your actual services, your towns, and your business name. It is not templated or generic.

Can I see the content before it posts?

Every piece of content goes through a client review step before it publishes. You get a review link, you approve or request a change. Nothing posts without your sign-off.

What if my services or service area changes?

Just let us know. We update your Legacy Brain and the new information feeds into all future content immediately.

What happens to my content data if I cancel?

You own it. Your Legacy Brain data, your images, your past posts — all of it is yours. We provide your files on request.
