Two Ways We Work With Your Accounts

Whether you have existing accounts or need us to start from scratch — we have a path for you.

Path A
We Handle Everything

For non-tech clients. We build everything on your behalf.

  • 1 KPW creates your Facebook Business Page on your behalf
  • 2 KPW creates + verifies your Google Business Profile (handles verification calls, postcard requests)
  • 3 KPW cleans up any duplicate or outdated listings
  • 4 You receive owner/admin invite via email
  • 5 KPW stays as Manager for ongoing service
  • 6 On cancellation: KPW removes itself within 48 hours. You keep 100% of accounts.

Add Us As Manager

For clients with existing accounts. We jump in and optimize.

  • 4 Same 48-hour removal on cancellation
