About This Blog

Questions about what you'll find here.

What do you write about on here?

Mostly the questions I get from business owners before they call — does my business need a website, what’s the difference between a one-page and a full site, what does Google Business actually do. Real answers without the marketing fluff.

Are these tips specific to Kansas businesses?

That’s the idea. A lot of what’s written about web design and SEO is written for businesses in big cities with big budgets. Central Kansas is different — the market is smaller, word of mouth still matters, and what works in Chicago doesn’t always work in Salina. I write from that perspective.

How often do you post new content?

When I have something worth saying. I’d rather post one useful article a month than fill the blog with content that doesn’t help anyone. Quality over volume.

Can I share these posts with other business owners?

Please do. That’s exactly what they’re for. Tag us on Facebook or just send the link.

I read the blog but still have questions — what do I do?

Call or text 785-577-7695 or fill out the contact form. Reading the blog is a good starting point but every business situation is a little different. Happy to talk through yours.

Do I have to hire KPW to use the information on this blog?

Not at all. Read as much as you want, use it however it helps. If you decide we’re the right fit to build or manage your online presence, great. If not, hopefully the posts were still useful.
