Common Questions

Quick answers to what Kansas business owners ask most.

Do I own my website and accounts?

Yes — with clear boundaries. Your domain name, Google Business Profile, and Facebook Business Page are always yours. If you ever move on, we remove our admin access within 48 hours — no holdups, no drama. The website files we build are yours to host. What stays with KPW is our build system and template workflow — that’s our internal process, not something we hand off. But everything your customers see and interact with? That belongs to you.

How long does it take to get set up?

Most websites are live within 7–14 business days after your intake call. Google Business Profile verification can take 5–10 business days. Facebook setup is typically done within 48 hours of receiving your intake information.

What if I already have some accounts or lost access to them?

That's very common — we handle it. We can recover lost Google Business Profiles, clean up duplicate listings, and take over management of existing Facebook pages. Just tell us what you have (or had) during the free consultation call.
