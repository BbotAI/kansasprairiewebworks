Common Questions

What if I already have a domain name?

Perfect — we'll use it. We'll work with whatever registrar you have (GoDaddy, Namecheap, Google Domains, anywhere) to connect your domain to your new site. You stay in control of your domain at all times.

How long does it take to build?

Most Tier 1 websites are ready for your review in 5–7 business days from when you complete the intake form. Tier 4 multi-page sites typically take 10–14 business days. We'll give you a clear timeline at the start of your project.

Do I need to provide my own content and photos?

We'll ask for whatever you have — photos of your work, your business, your team. If you don't have photos yet, we can use professional stock images as a starting point. We write or refine all the copy based on your intake form answers. You just review and approve.

What if I want to make changes after launch?

Tier 1 one-time includes 1 free revision after launch. Monthly clients get ongoing changes included — just call or text us. Additional revisions for one-time clients are available at $75/hour. Most small changes (hours, phone number, a new photo) take less than 30 minutes.
