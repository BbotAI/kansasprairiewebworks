Serving Central Kansas

Local web design, Google setup, Facebook pages, and digital marketing for small businesses across the Kansas prairie.

Our Primary Service Area

We're based in Salina, Kansas. Our primary service area covers Saline County and the surrounding communities within 60–90 miles. For everything we do, we focus on the towns and businesses of Central Kansas first.

Salina McPherson Abilene Bennington Concordia Junction City Manhattan

Where We Work

Every community in Central Kansas where small businesses are building their online presence.

Salina

Saline County

Our home base. Salina is the commercial hub of Central Kansas and home to thousands of small businesses — from trades and services to restaurants, retail, and professional offices. If you're in Salina, you're our neighbor, and we're proud to serve you first.

McPherson

McPherson County

McPherson is a thriving agricultural and industrial community with a growing small business scene. About 30 miles south of Salina on I-135, it's close enough for in-person meetings and deeply in our service area. Many McPherson businesses are in the market for better local visibility.

Abilene

Dickinson County

The birthplace of President Dwight D. Eisenhower, Abilene draws both tourism and local commerce. Small businesses here range from historic downtown shops to agricultural services serving Dickinson County. Getting found online in Abilene starts with a professional web presence.

Bennington

Ottawa County

Bennington is a small community in Ottawa County northeast of Salina. Rural Kansas businesses in smaller towns have unique needs — fewer competitors to beat out in local search, but also less visibility if they're not online at all. We help Bennington businesses show up where customers are looking.

Concordia

Cloud County

Concordia serves as the county seat of Cloud County and a regional center for North Central Kansas. Businesses here serve the surrounding agricultural communities and towns. A professionally built website and a strong Google listing can be the difference between getting a call and getting passed over.

Junction City

Geary County

Junction City is home to a large military and civilian business community near Fort Riley. The population is diverse and mobile — people move in and out regularly, which means they search online for local businesses before establishing roots. A strong web presence here has real competitive impact.

Manhattan

Riley County

Home to Kansas State University, Manhattan has a young, educated, and highly connected population that does nearly everything online first. Whether you serve the university community, the local K-State fan base, or the surrounding Flint Hills residents, your online presence has to be sharp and easy to find.

Anywhere in Kansas

While our core focus is Central Kansas, we work with businesses statewide. Web design and digital marketing are location-flexible — we've worked with clients from Wichita to Topeka to small towns in Western Kansas. If you're in Kansas and need help getting online, call us.
