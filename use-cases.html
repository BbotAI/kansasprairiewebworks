Common Questions

Do you build this exact system for every client, or is it customized?

The pipeline structure is the same — intake, image upload, topic research, drafting, review, publish — but what feeds it is different for every business. Mike’s Services runs off septic and excavation work in six counties. Yours would run off your actual services, your actual towns, and your actual photos. Same engine, different fuel.

How long does it take to get a system like this running for my business?

The intake form takes about 15 minutes, and most clients see their first live content within the first couple weeks of signing up. After that, it runs on its own weekly and monthly cycle — you’re not waiting on us to “get started” more than once.

Do I have to review everything before it goes live, or can it run fully automatic?

Every piece of content goes to you for approval before it posts — that step doesn’t get skipped. If you’d rather hand off more of that review down the road, we can talk about it, but nothing publishes without your sign-off unless you tell us otherwise.

Is my business’s data and photos private, or shared with other clients?

Private. Your Legacy Brain, your images, your service details — that data is yours and stays tied to your account. It’s never pooled with or used for another client’s content.

Can I see this level of detail for my own account once I’m a client?

Yes. What’s on this page is the public version — real examples shown with client permission. Once you’re set up, your client portal shows you your own pipeline: what’s queued, what’s posted, and what’s waiting on your review.
