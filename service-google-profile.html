What If I Lost My Account?

This happens more than you'd think. Someone set up the listing years ago with an email you no longer have access to. Or a previous employee claimed it. Or you simply don't remember the login.

The recovery process varies depending on whether the profile is:

  • Unclaimed (easiest — we claim it fresh)
  • Claimed with an accessible email (we help you recover access)
  • Claimed with an inaccessible email (requires Google's Business Profile ownership appeal)

We'll evaluate your specific situation on the free call and tell you exactly what the path forward looks like.

Common Questions

Does Google Business Profile cost money?

No — the Google Business Profile itself is completely free. You pay us to set it up correctly and optimize it. There are no ongoing Google fees for a basic listing.

How long until I show up in Google searches?

Once verified, Google typically indexes your profile within 1–2 weeks. Getting into the top local pack results takes longer and depends on your reviews, competition, and how well your profile is optimized — which is exactly why professional setup matters.

What if I lost access to my old Google listing?

We can help navigate Google's ownership appeal process. Outcomes vary based on your situation — unclaimed profiles are simple, previously-claimed profiles with lost credentials require filing an ownership request with Google. We'll walk you through exactly what to expect on the free call.

Will you be added as an owner of my Google profile?

No — we're added as a Manager, not an Owner. You always remain the Owner. If you ever cancel our services, we remove ourselves within 48 hours and you keep everything. Your profile is yours.
